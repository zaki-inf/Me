About nfoLab

Founded in 2014, Ai Medical InFo – Journal of Scientific Research is an open-access, peer-reviewed publication dedicated to disseminating cutting-edge science across disciplines. We believe knowledge should be freely available to all, regardless of institutional affiliation.

Our editorial team is committed to rigorous methodology, reproducibility, and transparency. All published datasets and code are required to be publicly archived.
